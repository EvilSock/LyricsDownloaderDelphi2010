<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
"Love Yourself" was written by Ed Sheeran, Benny Blanco and Justin Bieber.
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
According to Bieber "Love Yourself" is <i>"definitely about someone in my past, someone who I don't want to put on blast,"</i> he said the lyrics are <i>"cool because so many people can resonate with that, because how many women do we bring back, that mom doesn't really necessarily like?"</i>
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
In another interview Bieber revealed details about the song: <i>"It's just me and a guitar."</i> About Ed Sheeran's lyrics, he said: <i>"I think he's one of the most talented writers in the game right now, so just to have his input and his stories and our stories and match them up together and say 'What have you been through?,' and telling the same story."<i>
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
"Love Yourself" was nominated for two Grammy Awards: Song of the Year and Best Pop Solo Performance. It was the seventh best selling song of 2016 in the US.
</div>
