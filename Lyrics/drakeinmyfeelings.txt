<!-- song facts -->
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
This song features additional vocals by a duo <a href="https://www.azlyrics.com/c/citygirls.html">City Girls</a>. They are mentioned as 'Resha and J.T. later on.
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
This track is the first collaboration between Drake and producer TrapMoneyBenny.
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
Kiki from the first verse of this song is a reference to Drake's first girlfriend, singer <a href="https://www.azlyrics.com/c/chante.html#3815">Keshia Chanté</a>. They dated back in Toronto when they were teenagers.
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
"In My Feelings" contains a sample from <a href="https://www.azlyrics.com/lyrics/lilwayne/lollipop.html">"Lollipop"</a> performed by <a href="https://www.azlyrics.com/l/lilwayne.html">Lil Wayne</a> and "Smoking Gun" performed by Magnolia Shorty.
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
The line "From the block like you Jenny" is a reference to <a href="Jennifer">Jennifer Lopez's</a> song <a href="https://www.azlyrics.com/lyrics/jenniferlopez/jennyfromtheblock.html">"Jenny from the Block"</a>. She and Drake allegedly were in a relationship in 2017.
</div>
<div class="panel album-panel noprint">
At the end of this song the seventh episode of popular TV show Atlanta is sampled  where one of the girls wanted to take a selfie with Drake at a party. The episode was written and directed by Donald Glover (<a href="https://www.azlyrics.com/c/childishgambino.html">Childish Gambino</a>).
</div>
